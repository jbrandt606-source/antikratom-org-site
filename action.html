Why Action Is Urgent

Every day kratom remains on gas station shelves, another teenager tries it, another parent loses a child, another family is destroyed. The industry counts on you doing nothing. They have lobbyists, consultants, and paid scientists. All we have is the truth – and your voice.

The industry's mantra: "Let's study it more." Meanwhile, thousands suffer. We don't need more studies – we need you to pick up the phone, send an email, and demand action.

⛔ If We Do Nothing

  • More kids will be hospitalized. Poison control calls are rising – toddlers, teens, young adults.

  • Legislators will stay confused. Without hearing from you, they'll listen to well‑paid lobbyists instead.

How to Make Your Voice Count

  • Email your legislator: Use our template to tell them kratom is an unapproved, dangerous drug being sold to kids.
  • Write a testimony: Submit written or oral testimony for upcoming hearings. We provide sample language.
  • Download flyers: Print and distribute one‑page handouts at community meetings, school boards, churches.
  • Contact your state board of pharmacy: Demand they enforce existing laws against adulterated products.
  • Contact your state health department: Ask them to investigate poisonings and issue public warnings.
  • Reach out to medical societies: Urge them to take a position – doctors' voices carry weight with legislators.

Every action counts. The industry is counting on apathy. Prove them wrong.
